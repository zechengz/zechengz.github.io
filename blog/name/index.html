My name.

Following is the my Chinese name characters written by the most famous Chinese calligrapher Xizhi Wang (303 - 361). The charaters are Zhang, Ze and Cheng from the left to the right.

(I captured them from Xizhi’s different script works and concatenated them into one image)

The meaning of my name in Chinese characters:

  • 张Zhang: My family name was originated more than two thousand years ago and now there are more than 80,000,000 people with this family name.
  • 泽Ze: The first character of my given name which means water and nourishing all things.
  • 成Cheng: The second character of my given name which means success.
